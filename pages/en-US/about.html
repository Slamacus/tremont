Our Story

When we opened Trémont Restaurant in 2005 our vision was to create the best dining experience in Paris. In a city with endless options for bars and restaurants we found it challenging to find the restaurant with relaxing ambiance and the food quality we aim to provide. We wanted to provide the people of Paris with the original form of social networking: fun and quality experience.

Our belief in providing exceptional products with friendly service in a clean and welcoming setting had helped us earn the reputation among the locals as one of the best restaurants in Paris.
