Our Story

When we opened Tremont Restaurant in 2005 our vision was to create the best dining experience in Paris. In a city with endless options for bars and restaurants, we found it challenging to find the restaurant with relaxing ambient and the quality food we aim to offer. We wanted to provide the people of Paris with the original form of social networking: fun and quality dining experience.

Our belief is in providing exceptional quality products with friendly service in a clean and welcoming setting had helped us earn the reputation among the locals as one of the best restaurants in Paris.
